Prompt Box 1: Core Framework Analysis

Please read and understand the text given below. It's a process of solving a problem. 

Job gives me money.
Money gives me peace.

In this analogy:

- "Job represents the process — the effort or action taken."
- "Money represents the direct and visible output of that process."
- "Peace represents the impact — the deeper, often invisible, outcome produced by that output."

Using this framework, please answer the following questions:

1, What transformation does it recommend to its readers/users — in other words, what shift does it want them to make from their current challenge (the problem) to a desired real situation (the solution or new state)?
2, What role does it play in that transformation?
3, What is the process that enables this transformation?
4, What is the direct output of that process — the tangible or visible result?
5, What are the impacts of that output — the invisible or deeper effects it creates?

Prompt Box 5: Content Strategy

1. Extract key details from the document,
2. Identify the main product, ideal audience, and their problems,
3. Suggest 3–5 core topics I can consistently engage with to build visibility and authority.
4, Give me 10–12 of the most commonly used keywords I should search for on social media to find posts I can consistently engage with. These keywords should help me build visibility and authority by putting me in front of my ideal audience—without giving a long or overly detailed list.

Prompt Box 6: Detailed Analysis (P-P-S)

Treat the given text as a concept or product I want to sell. Analyze it and answer the following three points clearly and separately:

1, People: Who is the ideal target audience for this concept/product?
2, Problem: What specific problem of that audience does this concept/product solve?
3, Solution: What solution does this concept/product provide, and why is that solution effective? (No need to explain how the solution works—focus only on what the solution is and why it works.)

Prompt Box 8: Challenges Analysis

Implementation challenges and Post-solution/use-state challenges (additional):

Treat the given text as a process, workflow, method, or solution, Please provide two bullet lists:

1. Challenges or issues users may face while implementing this process (implementation challenges).

2. Problems or challenges users may face when using the given solution or after the solution is in place (post-solution challenges).

Make the lists clear, concise, and non-overlapping.
Do not mix the two categories.
