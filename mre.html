Audience & Offer Analyzer

Define your expertise to generate a deep-dive market analysis prompt.

2. Generated Prompt

I want to identify the most valuable prospective buyers for my expertise so I can build a successful online career. Please analyze everything thoroughly and provide clear, specific, and actionable insights. My expertise and skills: [Insert your expertise] How I prefer to provide my expertise (delivery method and working style): [Insert how you shall provide it] Using the information above, please answer the following points in a detailed, structured, and example-rich way: 1. Prospective Buyer Segments (Target Audience Identification) * Which groups of people, professionals, or companies would benefit most from my expertise? * What roles, industries, and seniority levels do they belong to? * What responsibilities do they have? * Which ones best match my preferred delivery method? * Explain why each segment is a strong match. 2. Their Problems & Pain Points (Needs and Demands) * What specific problems, challenges, or inefficiencies do these buyers experience? * How do these problems affect their goals, income, or operations? * Which of these problems can I help solve based on my expertise and delivery style? * Prioritize the problems by urgency or impact. 3. Topics & Issues They Discuss Online and on LinkedIn (Searchable Keywords) Provide very precise and practical outputs. * What topics, issues, questions, and frustrations do they talk about online? * What do they post about, comment on, complain about, or seek help with? * Provide: * keywords * long-tail phrases * hashtags * search terms * discussion themes * example post titles * Make the list ready for LinkedIn & Google search. 4. Their Desired Outcomes & Requirements (What They Want to Buy) * What results or improvements are they actively seeking? * What expectations do they have from an expert like me? * What features or delivery styles do they prefer? * What types of solutions are they willing to pay for? 5. Opportunity Analysis (What Offer I Should Create) * Based on their needs and my skills, what types of offers or services should I create? * Include options: done-for-you, consulting, coaching, training, digital products, productized services. * Rank opportunities from highest to lowest potential and explain why. 6. FAQs & Objections * What questions would they ask before deciding to work with someone like me? * What concerns, doubts, or objections might they have? * What proof or reassurance would they need? 7. Key Accounts to Follow (Market Monitoring & Trend Awareness) * Provide 20 major accounts, influencers, companies, or thought leaders in my niche. * Include: * their names * a short description of why they matter * their LinkedIn profile URLs (or website/social links if not on LinkedIn) * These should represent the best sources to observe market trends, industry conversations, and buyer behavior. Please ensure your full response is: * Highly structured * Practical and actionable * Supported with examples * Focused on real behavior, not generic marketing theory * Directly useful for understanding my audience, their needs, and my market
