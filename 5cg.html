How to Restructure for Different Formats
(Simple Guide)

✔ 1. Storytelling → Long-form post, thread, or Reel script
Just expand each outline point into one or two sentences.
✔ 2. Value → Carousel, list post, carousel voiceover, step-by-step Reel
Value posts convert extremely well into visual formats.
✔ 3. Win → Case study post, “before/after” carousel, testimonial-style Reel
Transformation translates beautifully to social.
✔ 4. Hot Take → Single tweet, short post, Reel with bold intro
These formats LOVE tension hooks.
✔ 5. Question → Poll, comment prompt, story question sticker
Perfect for engagement-driven formats.
